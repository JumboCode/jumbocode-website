The Client

People Making a Difference “Promotes informed and responsible volunteerism by involving people in meaningful, one-time, hands-on work that meets the needs of local charities and by assisting companies and charities in building successful community involvement programs.” As part of this goal, they run science bowls and science competitions for middle/high schools.



The Project

This app was created to assist in the administrative tasks that make the science competitions run so smoothly. It will be used by the administrators to view volunteers and whether they have checked in/out yet, send assignment updates directly to a volunteer, and push important notifications to event staffers. The volunteers will use this app to check in, see their assignment, and check out.
