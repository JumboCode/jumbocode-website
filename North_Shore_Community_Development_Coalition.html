The Client

The North Shore CDC is primarily an affordable housing non-profit. They are based in Salem, MA and invent in over twenty other towns along the north shore. They housing developments are designed to be high quality, and to capture multiple levels of affordability. They have begun recruiting dozens of artists from around the world from a variety of backgrounds to paint murals on their apartment buildings in order to encourage tourists and other residents to visit these neighborhoods.



The Project

This app is a way for the North Shore CDC to promote and showcase these murals. It will give the locations of these murals as well as a description of the artists and their work. It is presented on a map, allowing visitors to easily find the murals that most interest them.
