Tufts Daily

The Client

The Tufts Daily is the student-run, financial independent paper of Tufts University. Running peices of traditional news, arts, and sports, the Daily covers not only the integral aspects of campus life, but also of the surrounding Medford and Somerville communities.



The Project

The organization not only publishes in print daily, but also maintains a website devoted to it's content. Wanting to build off their current online presence, Jumbocode is developing an app for the paper so mobile users can acess content in a user friendly way.
